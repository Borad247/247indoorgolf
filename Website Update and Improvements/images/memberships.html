Membership Questions

Members receive a private access code that gives them direct entry to the facility at any time — day, night, weekends, and holidays. There are no staffed hours required. You come when you want, practice, and leave.
Members can use the member booking portal at booking247indoorgolf.com to reserve their preferred time slot. Walk-ins are welcome when bays are available.
Your own golf clubs and appropriate footwear (golf shoes or sneakers — no metal spikes). You're welcome to bring food and beverages.
