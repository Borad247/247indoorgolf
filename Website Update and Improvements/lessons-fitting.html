Club Fitting

The Right Clubs Make Everything Easier

Playing equipment that doesn't match your swing is like driving with the wrong prescription glasses — you're working twice as hard to compensate. A proper fitting fixes that.

We use Foresight Sports launch monitors to measure your actual ball flight with different clubs, shafts, and specs. The data tells us exactly what you need — not guesswork, not a salesman's opinion.

Driver & Woods

Optimize loft, shaft flex, and head design for maximum distance and accuracy.

Irons

Shaft weight, flex, lie angle, and length dialed in to your swing speed and tempo.

Wedges

Bounce, grind, and loft combinations for your typical course conditions and turf.

Putters

SAM Putt Lab analysis to match putter length, lie, and weight to your stroke.

Technology

The Tools We Use

The same technology used by touring professionals — available to every 247 member and lesson student.

Foresight Sports GCQuad

The industry gold standard for launch monitor accuracy. Measures ball speed, spin, launch angle, path, and face data with camera-based precision.

SAM Putt Lab

Measures your putting stroke in 3D — face angle, path, tempo, impact point, and consistency. Identify and fix the exact flaw in your putting stroke.

Puttview Green Technology

Our 1,200 sf putting green uses Puttview to project break lines and aim points directly onto the green surface. Practice the exact putt you'll face on the course.

Video Swing Analysis

High-speed video gives you and your coach a frame-by-frame look at your swing. Stop feeling the problem — see it, fix it, confirm it's fixed.
