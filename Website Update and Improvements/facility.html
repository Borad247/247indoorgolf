Simulator Bays

Foresight Sports — The Industry Standard

Foresight Sports is the choice of PGA Tour professionals, top instructors, and leading club fitters worldwide. Their GCQuad launch monitor captures data from four high-speed cameras, providing unmatched accuracy for both practice and fitting.

Each of our five bays runs the FSX Play software, giving you access to hundreds of courses, practice ranges, skills challenges, and competitive modes.

  • Ball speed, spin, launch angle, path, face angle
  • Hundreds of world-famous courses to play
  • Practice modes: range, target, skills challenges
  • Ideal for both practice and club fitting sessions

Short Game

1,200 sf Putting Green with Puttview

Our putting green is one of a kind in the area. The Puttview system projects the optimal aim line and break directly onto the green surface — you see exactly where to aim and how much break to play before you putt.

Paired with the SAM Putt Lab, you can analyze your stroke mechanics in 3D — face angle at address and impact, stroke path, tempo, and impact point consistency — then immediately practice with visual feedback on the green.

SAM Putt Lab

Measures your putting stroke in 3D with precision sensors. Identify the exact mechanical flaw holding back your putting and track your improvement over time.
